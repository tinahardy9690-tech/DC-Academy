Quick Answers

Click the DC Generator card on your dashboard. The tool will walk you through selecting a dispute type, entering the account details, and generating a personalized letter. Download as PDF and send directly to the bureau.
The DC Tool Kit includes an updated bureau address database. Always use certified mail with return receipt requested. Addresses for Equifax, Experian, and TransUnion dispute departments are kept current inside the toolkit.
Under the FCRA, credit bureaus must investigate and respond within 30 days (45 days if you provide additional information). If they don't respond in time, the item must be removed. Module 2 covers this in full.
Click the "Book Free Session" button on your dashboard or in the right sidebar. You'll be taken to the scheduling calendar to pick a time that works for you. Sessions are 30 minutes and completely free for active members.
